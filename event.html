About Blind Guardian

You can't get far into the power metal scene without hearing of Blind Guardian. With their first creation dating back to 1985, Blind Guardian stand solidly as a member of the genre, with their formidable and recognizable technical musicianship, symphonic layering and conceptual approach to album arrangement and composition. See the energy live on their North American tour.
